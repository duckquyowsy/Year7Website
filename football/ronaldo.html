Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo, also known as CR7 is a world famous footballer who currently plays for Al Nassr FC. He is portuguese and is rivalled with Lionel Messi as one of the best players in the world. He has never won a world cup but he has won 5 champion leagues. He started off his career in Sporting CF and then he went to Manchester United. From there he moved to Real Madrid CF and then he went to Juventus in Serie A only to go back to Manchester United to then go to Al Nassr FC. His most famous goal is his incredible bicycle kick he scored against Juventus while playing for Real Madrid.
