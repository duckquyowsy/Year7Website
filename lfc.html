Liverpool F.C

Liverpool F.C is a football club located in Liverpool, England. The club's home stadium is called 'Anfield' with a seating capacity of around 54,000 people. One of their most famous nicknames is 'The Reds', sounds similar to their rivals', Manchester United, which is 'The Red Devils'. The club was founded in 1892, but the Premier League was only created in 1992. Their biggest win was 11-0 against a club named Strømsgodset in 1974, but their biggest league win was out of three; 10-1 against Rotherham Town, 9-0 against Crystal Palace, or 9-0 against Bournemouth. In total, the club has won 19 league titles, 7 FA Cups, 9 League Cups, 6 European Cups, 1 FIFA Club World Cup, 2 Women’s Super League Titles, 8 FA Cups, 9 League Cups, 3 UEFA Cups, 4 UEFA Super Cups, 16 Charity/Community Shields, and 4 FA Youth Cups.
