Liverpool F.C

Liverpool F.C is a football club located in Liverpool, England. Currently, they are in the Premier League and have won 19 titles; 1 Premier League, and 18 others. The club's home stadium is called 'Anfield' with a seating capacity of around 54,000 people. One of their most famous nicknames is 'The Reds', sounds similar to their rivals', Manchester United, which is 'The Red Devils'. The club was founded in 1892, but the Premier League was only created in 1992. Their biggest win was 9-0 against a club named Bournemouth, but their biggest win against one of their rivals, was 7-0 against Man U in the 22/23 Premier League season.
